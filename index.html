About us

EliteNotes is all about taking notes. We know very well the amount of time and focus you indulge in every single day to capture key information out of any conversation. Welcome to EliteNotes, your next virtual assistant which enables A.I to automate your entire process of note-taking and let you build notes without any human intervention.

Transcription
